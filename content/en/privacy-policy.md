### Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use Cookies and other cool tracking tech to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and store some info. These tracking tools include beacons, tags, and scripts ‚Äì they help us gather and track data, and ultimately, make our Service even better. Here's a peek at the tech we might use:

- **Cookies (or Browser Cookies)**: These are just tiny files that land on your device. You can totally tell your browser to say 'nope' to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's coming. But, fair warning, if you block 'em, some parts of our Service might not work right. Unless you've specifically told your browser to reject Cookies, our Service will likely be using them.
- **Web Beacons**: You might find these tiny electronic files (also called clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs) tucked into some parts of our Service or our emails. They let us do cool things like count how many folks visited a page or opened an email, and help us gather other website stats (like seeing how popular a section is or making sure our systems are running smoothly).

Cookies come in two main flavors: 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. Persistent ones stick around on your computer or phone even after you're offline, but Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for a few reasons, which we'll break down for you:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for making sure you can actually use the services on our Website and access all its cool features. They help us confirm who you are and stop any funny business with user accounts. Seriously, without these, the services you want just wouldn't work, so we only use them for that essential stuff.
- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply remember if you've already given us the thumbs-up to use Cookies on the Website.
- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are neat because they help us remember your choices when you're using the Website ‚Äì like keeping you logged in or remembering your language preference. The whole point is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to keep typing in your preferences every single time you visit. Pretty handy, right?

Want to know more about the Cookies we use and what your options are? Just pop over to our Cookies Policy or check out the Cookies section right here in our Privacy Policy.
