**Score: 82 (Infrastructure/Inference Acceleration)**

**Commentary**: This is a classic case of "engineering optimization changing the research experience." For companies needing to generate tens of thousands of protein sequences in bulk, this directly translates to halving their computational costs. Sweet! üëç
